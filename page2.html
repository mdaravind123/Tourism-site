Favourite Places

Taj Mahal

If there was just one symbol to represent all of India, it would be the Taj Mahal. The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble built by Mughal emperor, Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

Golden Temple

Amritsar is world-famous for the beautiful and highly reserved Golden Temple and is spiritually the most significant shrine in Sikhism. It became a centre of the Singh Sabha Movement.

Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace is a historical palace and the royal residence also known as Amba Vilas Palace. It used to be the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and the seat of the Kingdom of Mysore.
